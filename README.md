# 🛒 Shopify Sales & Customer Funnel Report | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Shopify sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, retention metrics, payment methods, and product category performance using an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard provides business insights into revenue trends, customer engagement, regional sales distribution, and product performance to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of sales and customer data daily. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to understand:

* Sales growth trends
* Customer retention performance
* Repeat purchase behavior
* Top-performing products
* Preferred payment methods
* Regional sales distribution

This dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key performance indicators and identify business opportunities.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Generated over $4M in total net sales.
* Achieved a 46% repeat customer rate.
* Shopify Payments contributed the highest share of revenue.
* Running Shoes and Tennis Shoes were top-performing product categories.
* Certain provinces and cities significantly outperformed others in sales generation.
* Customer lifetime value exceeded $943 per customer.
